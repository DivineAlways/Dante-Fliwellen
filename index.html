What is TaskFlow?

TaskFlow is a web application designed to help small business owners and freelancers streamline their workflow. With TaskFlow, you can easily:

  • Add, edit, and mark tasks as complete.
  • Organize tasks into projects for better oversight.
  • View progress reports and analytics.
  • Manage your profile and preferences.

Our goal is to provide a clear, simple, and clean interface that minimizes distraction and maximizes productivity.

This is a wireframe prototype for TaskFlow. For project documentation or additional notes, please refer to the Project Documentation.
